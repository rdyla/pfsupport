Common Questions

We've Got Answers

A few things customers typically ask when something like this happens.

Do I need to do anything right now?
No — nothing is required. Your existing bookmark will redirect you automatically. The only optional step is updating your bookmark to support.packetfusion.com so you're always going to the new address directly.
How do I log in? Has the sign-in process changed?
Yes — and it's actually simpler and more secure than before. The new portal uses email verification instead of a traditional password. Here's how it works:

1. Go to support.packetfusion.com and enter your work email address.
2. You'll receive a one-time code in your inbox — sent by Cloudflare, our secure access provider.
3. Enter that code to sign in. That's it — no password needed.
●
A security upgrade, not a hurdle. By moving to one-time email codes, your account is protected even if a password were ever compromised. There's nothing to remember and nothing to reset — just check your inbox each time you log in. The email will always come from Cloudflare on behalf of Packet Fusion.
Are my old cases and ticket history still there?
Yes. Every case — open, in progress, or resolved — is fully intact on the new portal. All attachments, notes, and correspondence are there. Nothing was migrated away or deleted.
Why did Packet Fusion move to a new portal?
We moved the portal to our own domain — support.packetfusion.com — as part of a broader investment in our support infrastructure. Hosting the portal ourselves gives us more control over performance, security, and future improvements. The experience you see today is the same, but this move sets us up to make it better over time.
What if my old bookmark stops working one day?
The old portal address will be retired on April 30, 2026. After that date it will no longer redirect. To ensure uninterrupted access, we recommend updating your bookmark to support.packetfusion.com as soon as possible. You can also always find the portal link at packetfusion.com/support.
I'm having trouble logging in — what should I do?
First, make sure you're going to support.packetfusion.com and entering your work email address. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a one-time code from Cloudflare — it typically arrives within a minute. If you're still having trouble, email us at info@packetfusion.com or call 925-701-2020 and our team will get you sorted out quickly.
Still Have Questions?
Our support team is available and happy to help with anything related to the portal migration or your account. Reach us by email or phone — we'll take care of you.
